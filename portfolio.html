The map of Italy discusses issues of religion and immigration in Italy in 2015. Within the population, immigrants amounted to only 8 percent, and the majority of immigrants were Muslim or Jewish. Curiously, there are only 8 mosques found in all of Italy, and the nation holds the 3rd highest population of Muslims in all of Europe behind France and England suggesting a silent mistreatment of other religions. This map was made in class for GEOG 370.

This project details the issues that are currently affecting museums and museum work. It discusses the curation crisis, which has affected the way museums are able to manage, preserve, and curate collections. The main problem that is contributing to this curation crisis is a lack of funds for museums. Therefore, it also takes two case studies into consideration and compares their finances with how their collections are being managed. These two case studies are the Milwaukee Public Museum and the American Museum of Natural History. https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/f2ec22adf0d64bf4887fde497ba3bcdb
